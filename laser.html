Laser Cutting

The Laser Cutting module in our FAB LAB provides you with the opportunity to learn and create intricate designs using laser technology. This documentation will guide you through the process of using the laser cutting machine effectively. Laser cutting is a precise and efficient method of cutting various materials using a laser beam. In this module, you'll learn how to design your projects and use the laser cutter to bring your creations to life.

## Safety Guidelines
- Always wear appropriate safety gear, including protective eyewear.
- Follow all lab rules and guidelines for laser cutter operation.
- Keep the work area clean and organized to prevent accidents.
- Never leave the laser cutter unattended while it's in operation.

## Materials The laser cutter can be used on various materials, including:
- Acrylic
- Wood
- Cardboard

## Preparing Your Design
1. Create or import your design using vector design software like Adobe Illustrator or CorelDRAW.
2. Ensure that your design is properly sized and positioned within the cutting area.
3. Convert text to outlines to prevent font compatibility issues.
